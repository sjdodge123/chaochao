Privacy Policy

Chao Chao · Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Chao Chao ("the Game", "we", "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to the Game wherever you play it — on the web at chaochaogame.com, embedded in partner sites (CrazyGames, Poki, itch.io), and as a Discord Activity.

Chao Chao is operated by Steven Dodge. If you have questions, contact us at sjdodge@outlook.com.

The short version. We collect the minimum needed to run a multiplayer game and save your progress: a sign-in identity (only if you choose to sign in), your in-game progression and cosmetics, and anonymous gameplay analytics. We do not sell your data. You can play as a guest with no account. In the Discord Activity we read who is talking to show a speaking indicator — we never access or record audio.

1. Information we collect

a. Account & identity (only if you sign in)

You can play entirely as a guest. If you choose to sign in, we receive:

  • Discord (web sign-in or the Discord Activity): your Discord user ID, username, global/display name, and avatar; and — with your consent — the list of servers you are in (used only to group players from the same server into a match). Provided via Discord's OAuth2 / Embedded App SDK.
  • Google (web sign-in): your name and profile picture from Google OAuth.
  • A randomly generated device identifier stored in your browser to link your progress to your account and recognize returning devices.

We do not receive or store your Discord/Google password. We do not publicly display your email address.

b. Gameplay & progression

For signed-in players we store a progression record: experience points and level, unlocked and equipped cosmetics, lifetime medal/achievement counts, win counts, and the device identifiers associated with the account. Guests' progress is not saved on our servers.

c. Usage & performance analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (with BigQuery export) to understand how the Game is played and to improve performance. This includes gameplay events (e.g. matches started/ finished, sign-ins), coarse device/performance signals (input method, rendering tier), and the embedding host (e.g. Discord, a partner site). Analytics is tied to a pseudonymous identifier, not to your real name. Analytics is disabled / runs as a no-op inside the Discord Activity.

d. Content you create or share

  • Display name & avatar shown to other players in a match.
  • In-game emotes/expressions you send during play.
  • Map submissions from the in-game map editor. A submitted map is published publicly as a pull request to our open-source GitHub repository and, if accepted, ships in the Game.
  • Feedback / bug reports you send through the in-game widget, including any email address, page, and diagnostic context you choose to include. These are filed as issues in our GitHub repository.

e. Technical & connection data

To run real-time multiplayer and protect the service we process your IP address (for region/datacenter and anti-abuse checks) and basic connection diagnostics. We do not use this to build advertising profiles, and we do not retain raw IP addresses long-term.

f. Voice activity (Discord Activity only)

When you launch Chao Chao as a Discord Activity, with the rpc.voice.read scope you grant, we read voice-activity state from Discord — i.e. which participant is currently speaking — to show an in-game "speaking" indicator. We do not access, transmit, record, or store any audio.

2. How we use your information

  • Run the Game and real-time multiplayer matches.
  • Save and restore your progression and cosmetics across sessions and devices.
  • Match you into rooms (including grouping a Discord voice channel into one match).
  • Detect and prevent cheating, abuse, and automated/bot traffic.
  • Measure usage and performance to improve the Game.
  • Respond to your feedback and bug reports.

3. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR/UK GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (to provide the Game you requested), legitimate interests (security, anti-abuse, and product improvement), and consent (e.g. the Discord scopes you approve, and analytics where consent is required).

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not "sell" or "share" it as those terms are defined under California law. We share it only with service providers that help us run the Game, each under its own terms:

ProviderPurpose
DiscordActivity platform, sign-in, voice-activity state, presence (governed also by Discord's Privacy Policy)
SupabaseAuthentication and the progression database
Google AnalyticsUsage & performance analytics (web only)
Heroku / cloud hostingRunning the game server
CloudflareContent delivery / network
GitHubPublic map submissions and feedback issues

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.

5. Cookies & local storage

The Game uses browser local/session storage for essential functions: your device identifier, your sign-in session, and a local mirror of your cosmetic choices when playing as a guest. Web analytics may set cookies as described above. We do not use cross-site advertising cookies in the Discord Activity.

6. Data retention

We keep your progression record while your account is active. Analytics data is retained for up to 14 months. Public map submissions and feedback issues persist in our GitHub repository as part of the project's history. You can request deletion as described below.

7. Your rights & choices

  • Play entirely as a guest, providing no sign-in information at all.
  • Request access to, correction of, export of, or deletion of your account data by emailing sjdodge@outlook.com.
  • Revoke the Game's access from your Discord or Google account settings at any time.
  • Depending on your region (e.g. EEA/UK/California), you may have additional rights including the right to object, to restrict processing, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Children

Chao Chao is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by Discord and by your local law, whichever is higher). Do not use the Game if you are under that age. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted transport (HTTPS/WSS) and access-controlled server credentials. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. International transfers

Our providers may process data in the United States and other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, an in-game notice.
