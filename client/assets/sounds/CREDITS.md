# Sound credits

## Audience / crowd reactions

`crowd-cheer-big.mp3`, `crowd-cheer.mp3`, and `crowd-ooh.mp3` are short cues
trimmed and loudness-normalized from **"Free Crowd Cheering Sounds"** by
**Gregor Quendel**.
