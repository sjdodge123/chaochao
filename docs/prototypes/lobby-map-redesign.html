Design intent

  • Hub Plaza (west) — all four stations arc around the spawn pad on clean grass/dirt, arranged by priority: Skins and Bots (AI) hold the central spots flanking the road exit, while Playlist and Game Mode (room-wide, set-once) sit on the plaza outskirts.
  • Spinner Court (center) — a clean grass/dirt clearing around the start spinner; the grass road runs spawn → spinner → east testing grounds. The dirt ring around the court is freckled with four small ice patches for casual sliding without crowding the spinner itself.
  • Ability Alley (north strip) — bomb, speed-up, slow-down, and cut pickup pads consolidated on one grass strip, clear of both the plaza and the court.
  • Ice Rink (north-east) — the ice field moves to the corner, with ice-cannon pads at its entry, a goal tile at the far corner to practice sliding into a finish, and a lava pocket melted into its south edge (the lava–ice interaction).
  • Bumper Court + Splash Pool (east) — both bumpers (static + moving rail) live on a grass court; the water pool sits south-east of it, and its south rim runs into the lava springs and sand shore — a deliberate water + lava + sand tri-point that also demos the stone-seam wall on water–lava edges.
  • Lava Springs (south-east) — the lava lake keeps its corner with a full sand shore, a goal on the far sand as a risk/reward gauntlet, and the splash pool touching its north-east arc. Star Power pads sit on the western shore — grab one and the lava gauntlet becomes runnable.
  • Sand Drag (south strip) — a slow-tile strip for feeling drag, well below the court, with Speed-Up and Ice Cannon pads buried in the sand — boost against the drag, or pave ice over the slow ground (the idle reset heals it).
  • Ground scheme — dirt (normal) is the base field; grass (fast) marks the main road, hub plaza pads, spinner court, ability alley, and bumper court, so the fast tile literally reads as the path network.

What this fixes

  • Stations are no longer scattered across the whole map (skin NW, playlist far E, mode far NE, AI SW) — they centralize around spawn.
  • Spawn plaza loses the speed-buff pads, cut pads, and sand field that crowd it today.
  • The spinner loses its adjacent static bumper (540,384), water+lava pocket (~750–830, 400–470), and the mid-path ice field (~450–530, 300–505).
  • No interior void islands — one connected walkable field, so new players can't get visually "fenced".

Implementation notes

  • The spinner isn't map data — game.js spawns LobbyStartButton at world.center — so the map just keeps that area clean.
  • Stations and spawn pad are authored in the map JSON (stations[], spawnPad); hazards keep ids 900/901. Export produces a drop-in replacement for client/maps/_LobbyTutorial.json.
  • Ability pads use the real pickup tile ids (102 bomb, 104 speed+, 105 speed−, 107 ice cannon, 108 cut), so the lobby idle-reset keeps restoring them.
