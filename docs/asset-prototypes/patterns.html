chaochao — pattern painter prototype

17 equippable patterns, each a texture overlay that MUST tint to the player's color (paint). Pick any color (left) and every pattern re-tints live. Forward = +X. Patterns are drawn over a sample cart body and clipped to its silhouette, exactly like drawPatternOverlay in draw.js. (First 7 are the ladder/achievement set; the next 10 are extra options to choose from.)
